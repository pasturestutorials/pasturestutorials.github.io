Our Story

About Pastures Tutorial Centre

Empowering NOUN students in Abuja with quality education, realistic exam preparation, and personalized support since 2018.

Pastures Tutorial Centre is an Abuja-based tutorial hub dedicated to helping National Open University of Nigeria (NOUN) students excel in their studies.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between distance learning and academic excellence, we have grown to become one of Abuja's most trusted tutorial centres for NOUN students. Our approach combines structured learning, realistic exam simulations, and personalized attention.

We specialize in realistic mock examinations that mirror NOUN's CBT format for 100–200 level courses and pen-on-paper practice for higher levels. Our students don't just study — they practice exactly what they'll face on exam day.
